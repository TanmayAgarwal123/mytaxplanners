About Us

We are Certified Public Accountants (CPAs) and we do

MyTaxPlanners is an online tax preparation service specifically designed for non-resident students in the USA. Managed by CPAs in the USA, we provide a user-friendly interface, educational resources, and cost-effective solutions tailored to the unique needs of non-resident students. For individuals and businesses alike, dealing with the minutiae of accounting and financial planning consumes precious moments that could be spent elsewhere. Whether you want to spend more free time with friends and family or focus on strategic decision-making for your business, working with MyTaxPlanners can help.
Our team has been providing a wide array of financial management services since 2000, freeing clients up to enjoy the most rewarding parts of living their lives and running their businesses. Get in touch today to entrust your finances to us.

My services and Works

Consultancy

Consultancy involves providing expert advice and guidance to businesses on financial management, accounting practices, and strategic decision-making to optimize performance and achieve financial objectives.
Return Filing

Return filing refers to the process of submitting financial information and tax details to the government for assessment and compliance, typically done annually by individuals and businesses.
Tax Planning

Tax planning involves strategizing financial affairs to minimize tax liabilities legally and efficiently, utilizing deductions, credits, and exemptions to optimize tax outcomes while complying with tax laws and regulations.
